From a Problem to a Purpose

Working at an accounting firm meant constant travel and operating in spaces without designated desks. Client visits often offered minimal desk space—sometimes just small tables barely big enough for a laptop or two. I couldn’t always use my portable monitor because there simply wasn’t room.

Back at the central office, desks were available on a first-come, first-served basis, and they were often cramped. Associates started reserving meeting rooms with friends just to secure extra room for work. Watching my peers, I noticed them stacking lunch boxes, binders, or books behind their laptop screens to prop up portable monitors. And I found myself straining, turning my head back and forth between screens, feeling the toll on my neck. Coming from a software development background, I was used to the productivity boost of multiple screens. Switching constantly between windows felt like a setback.

When I started searching for a better solution, I expected to find something ready-made. I combed through popular and international sites but found nothing that met my needs. So, I began sketching my own design, intending initially to build a basic version for myself. But as my colleagues saw my plans, they began asking for one, too. This was the spark that drove me to create a professional version that I could be proud of, something people would genuinely want to use.

That was the beginning of a six-year journey. I had no prior knowledge of product design, manufacturing, or running a business. But night after night, I read, researched, and learned everything I needed. I went through seven prototypes, countless CAD revisions, and tested multiple materials to achieve the right balance of durability, lightness, and elegance. I wanted something compact, stylish, and above all, practical—a product that wouldn’t lose parts, could withstand the daily grind, and made users feel proud to use it.

After six years of dedication, I finally have a product to share, one that I believe will help others work more comfortably and effectively. This product was born from a real need, and it’s refined for your better days. I hope it brings you as much ease and efficiency as it’s brought me.
